Massage Therapy

A.K.A Medical massage therapy is a planned treatment with specific goals in mind. Your treatment will factor your physical needs, injuries, medical history, to promote good muscle funtion, healing, reduce stress, enhances muscle relaxation, improve local circulation, and introduce exercises that will allow you to build upon massage. In this we focus on three different directions. Sports Therapy. Occupational Therapy. Rehabilitation Therapy.
