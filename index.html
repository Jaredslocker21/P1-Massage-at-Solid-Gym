Massage Therapy

A.K.A Medical massage therapy. Is a planned treatment with very specific goals. Your treatment will factor your physical condition, injuries and medical history to promote good muscle function, healing, reduce stress, improve local circulation. We will also introduce exercises that will allow you to build upon your massage.
In this we focus on three different directions.
Sports Therapy. Occupational Therapy. Rehabilitation Therapy.
